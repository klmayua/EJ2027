# Demo Route Strategy
**Project:** EJ THE VOICE 2027
**Objective:** The "Confidence Path" for Stakeholders

## The "Golden Path" (Demo Sequence)

1. **The Hook (HomePage):** 
   - Start at the Hero. 
   - Scroll slowly through the 7 sections. 
   - *Emotional Goal:* "I recognize this man. This feels inevitable."

2. **The Depth (Timeline $\rightarrow$ Biography):**
   - Click "View the Journey".
   - Move through the life milestones.
   - Transition to Biography for the "human" details.
   - *Emotional Goal:* "He has the experience and the character."

3. **The Proof (Media Archive $\rightarrow$ Transparency):**
   - Move to the Archive. Show the broadcast history.
   - Transition to Transparency to show the "Service-first" approach.
   - *Emotional Goal:* "This is real. This is professional. This is honest."

4. **The Action (Community $\rightarrow$ Volunteer):**
   - Show the community structure.
   - Transition to the Volunteer page to show "how to help."
   - *Emotional Goal:* "I want to be part of this."

5. **The Closing (Contact):**
   - Final slide to the Contact page.
   - *Emotional Goal:* "This is accessible and open."

## Demo-Safe Guardrails
- **Avoid:** The `JoinPage` and `DonatePage` unless specifically asked. They are the highest risk for "SaaS energy."
- **Avoid:** Any "Coming Soon" pages. Stick to the Golden Path.
- **Safe Zones:** Keep the demo within the "Editorial Loop."
