# Mobile Emotional Audit
**Project:** EJ THE VOICE 2027
**Focus:** 320px $\rightarrow$ 768px emotional resonance

## Mobile Experience Analysis

### 1. Narrative Compression
- **The Scroll Rhythm:** The 7-section storyflow on `HomePage` translates well to mobile, but the "Recognition" hero is very tall. On small screens (320px), the user may feel "trapped" in the hero before seeing the value.
- **Spacing:** Vertical breathing room is generally good, but the `DonatePage` grid becomes a long, exhausting stack of cards.

### 2. Touch-Point Emotion
- **CTAs:** Mobile buttons are appropriately sized, but the "Join Arena Now" button on mobile feels too "app-like" and not "civic-like."
- **Navigation:** The `BottomNav` provides a great "OS" feel, but it competes with the `Navbar` on small screens.

### 3. Visual Density
- **Text blocks:** The `70ch` measure is respected, but on mobile, some paragraphs in `AboutPage` feel too dense.
- **Placeholders:** "Coming Soon" placeholders are particularly jarring on mobile as they take up the whole viewport.

## Recommendations
- Reduce Hero height on mobile (320px-390px) to bring the first narrative hook (Memory) into view faster.
- Soften the `DonatePage` mobile layout to feel like a "Letter of Intent" rather than a "Pricing Table."
- Ensure `BottomNav` has a consistent "active" state that feels like a physical switch.
