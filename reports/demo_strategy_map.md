**Phase:** DEMOABILITY_SPRINT
**Objective:** High-Confidence Stakeholder Walkthrough

## 1. The Golden Path (The "Confidence Loop")
The demo should follow this specific sequence to build emotional momentum.

| Sequence | Page | Narrative Beat | Emotional Target | Key Visual Anchor |

| 03 | **Biography** | "The Man" | Human Connection | Family/Faith details |

## 2. Perceived Completion Strategy
To avoid the "Coming Soon" void during a live demo, we will implement the following:

- **The "Focused Navigation" Guardrail:** In the demo-safe version, navigation will be curated to lead users toward the Golden Path.
- **The "Narrative Bridge":** For pages like `Vision` or `Accountability` (currently placeholders), we will transform them into "Editorial Manifestos" — short, powerful, single-page statements rather than empty dashboards.
- **Simulation Layers:** In the `Join` and `Donate` pages, we will replace "conversion funnels" with "Civic Registry" and "Impact Support" patterns.

## 3. "SaaS-to-Civic" Transition Map
We will surgically replace "Tech-Bro" patterns with "Civic" patterns.

| SaaS Pattern (FORBIDDEN) | Civic Pattern (MANDATORY) | Emotional Shift |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| "Join the Arena Now" | "Become Part of the Work" | From 'User' $\rightarrow$ 'Citizen' |
| "Bronze/Silver/Gold Tiers" | "Community/Regional/Anchor Support" | From 'Customer' $\rightarrow$ 'Supporter' |
| "Power Impact" (Neon) | "Support the Vision" (Gold/Navy) | From 'Hype' $\rightarrow$ 'Seriousness' |
| "Real-time Ledger" (Crypto) | "Institutional Transparency" (Editorial) | From 'Speculation' $\rightarrow$ 'Accountability' |
| "Unlock Rewards" | "Contribute Your Skills" | From 'Incentive' $\rightarrow$ 'Purpose' |

## 4. Demo Guardrails (The "No-Go" Zones)
To maintain the illusion of a finished, premium platform:
- **Avoid** clicking `DonatePage` until the "Tiers" are humanized.
- **Avoid** `JoinPage` until the "SaaS form" is converted to a "Civic Registry."
- **Avoid** `ContactPage` until the "Standard Blue" is replaced with "Platform Gold."

## 5. Stakeholder-Specific Hooks
- **For Donors:** Focus on the `Transparency` $\rightarrow$ `Volunteer` flow (Trust $\rightarrow$ Action).
- **For Media:** Focus on the `Timeline` $\rightarrow$ `Media Archive` flow (History $\rightarrow$ Reach).
- **For Strategic Allies:** Focus on the `Community` $\rightarrow$ `HomePage` flow (Base $\rightarrow$ Vision).
